## Who We Are

CABA-LABS is a software company based in Kingston, Jamaica, building infrastructure, business systems, and standalone applications — for clients and for the market.

We work across two tracks:

- **Infrastructure & Systems** — custom backend systems, internal tools, and business infrastructure built for companies that need software to run their operations reliably.
- **Products** — purpose-built applications developed in-house and sold directly, solving specific problems for specific markets.

## What We Build

- **Business Infrastructure** — backend systems, APIs, authentication, data pipelines, and the operational tooling companies need to run and scale.
- **Custom Business Systems** — tailored software solutions designed around how a specific business actually operates, not generic off-the-shelf tooling.
- **Commercial Applications** — standalone apps built to be sold, supporting real users and real transactions in production.

## Rooted in Kingston, Built for the World

CABA-LABS is headquartered in Kingston, Jamaica, but our work isn't limited by geography — we build infrastructure and applications for businesses and markets globally, bringing a perspective that off-the-shelf software often misses.


## Approach

- Production-grade software, not prototypes
- Systems built to be maintained, not just shipped
- Direct, practical solutions over unnecessary complexity
